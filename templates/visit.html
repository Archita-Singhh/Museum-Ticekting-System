Plan Your Visit

Location

Our museum is located at Janpath, New Delhi - 110011. It is easily accessible by metro, bus, and taxi.

Timings

The Museum is open to the public during the following hours:

  • Tuesday to Sunday: 10:00 AM to 6:00 PM
  • Closed on Mondays and National Holidays

Entry Fees

We offer affordable entry fees for all visitors:

  • Kids(0-5): ₹0
  • Growwn-ups(6-18): ₹15
  • Adults(19-59): ₹20
  • Seniors(60 and above): ₹10

Important Information

  • Visitors must carry a valid ID card for verification (especially for student discounts).
  • Food and beverages are not allowed inside the museum premises.
  • We recommend wearing comfortable shoes as there is a lot to explore within the museum.
  • Wheelchair access is available for visitors with disabilities.
